## About ABToolkit

ABToolkit, also known as Produk AB or Abimanyu Toolkit, is a local electronics
tools brand based in Blitar, East Java, Indonesia.

Founded around 2018 as a small home-based project, ABToolkit has continued
to develop practical, reliable, and affordable equipment for electronic
service technicians.

Our products are developed through continuous evaluation, field feedback,
and gradual improvements to ensure dependable daily use.

## What We Develop

ABToolkit develops tools and supporting software for electronic repair and
technical work, including:

- Soldering stations
- Hot air and rework stations
- Power supply and MBR equipment
- Embedded control systems
- Desktop monitoring and configuration applications
- Product manuals and technical support documentation

## Featured Project

### T210 Connect

Official desktop monitoring and configuration application for supported
ABToolkit T210 devices.

T210 Connect provides USB serial communication, real-time monitoring,
device configuration, and integration with supported ABToolkit hardware.
